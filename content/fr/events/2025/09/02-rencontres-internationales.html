Paris 2024 programme

The Paris event ran successfully from 18 to 24 November 2024. 5,163 people attended the programme which brought together 142 works from 52 countries in different venues: the Gaîté Lyrique, the Jeu de Paume, the CWB Paris, the Musée de la Chasse et de la Nature, the Forum des images, the Pathé Les Fauvettes and the Fiminco Foundation. Thank you all again for your presence and your enthusiasm!
